Voronoi

Product service system (PSS)

This project aims to deliver high-quality furniture products enhanced by additive manufacturing. The goal was to offer both aesthetic value and functional services, such as product updates and the replacement of individual parts—ultimately extending the product’s lifecycle
The design-driven research and planning process led to the development of a viable business model, including a system of key partners and resources. Leveraging available technologies, I created a furniture line based on the Voronoi structure to standardize the visual identity and composition of the collection.
