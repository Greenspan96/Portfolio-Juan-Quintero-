Packaging Design - Newprint

Structural design and prepress

As Structural Designer, I worked on a wide variety of projects, developing tailored packaging solutions for diverse industries such as food, beauty, and pharmaceuticals.
Creating custom packaging for each product required a high level of attention to detail and creativity to deliver functional, innovative, and production-feasible designs.(Prototyping with Kongsberg XE-10)
My responsibilities ranged from detailed structural design to ordering the necessary tools and materials, while maintaining constant communication with clients. Additionally, I contributed directly to production processes, including printing plate production, assisting with digital printing, and coordinating shipping.
