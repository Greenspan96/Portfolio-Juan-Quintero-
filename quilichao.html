Quilichao Service Design

User experience and service design

This project explores the design of a restaurant’s brand and service experience through a case study conducted in Bogotá. The objective was to articulate and strengthen the brand’s core values—culinary tradition and cultural diversity—across the entire customer journey.
Using service design and UX methodologies, including visual communication frameworks and a service blueprint, the project mapped key touchpoints, interactions, and support processes. This analysis informed strategic opportunities to improve brand consistency and optimize both frontstage customer interactions and backstage operational experiences.
